Areas of Interest

Things I like to work on.

Data Science

Data Scientist (n.): Person who is better at statistics than any software engineer and better at software engineering than any statistician. I acquire and clean data, extract insights, develop visualizations, and present any findings to lead business driven decisions. Short and simple!

 

Image Science

Image science is the study of the science, computing, and engineering theroies behind the technology that goes into creating images, the integration of this technology into imaging systems, and the application of those systems to gather information and solve scientific problems. What distinguishes me from others in this field is my professional background in optics, mathematics, physics, and programming. Whether it be image processing with raw images, assessing image quality, determining lens distortion, camera alignment, or something else entirely - I have 4 years of industry proven experience to solve it.

 

Computer Vision

I have 3 years of professional research experience in developing unique algorithms for intrinsic calibration of high-volume manufactured automotive cameras in Python and MATLAB. Additionally, I have developed and trained custom deep learning networks to detect unique camera targets in highly distorted images.
